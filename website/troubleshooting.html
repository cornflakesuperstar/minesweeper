Trouble-shooting:

1.
Q: My Best Times are not being stored
A: Your Best Times are stored in a "Cookie" on your computer. You need to enable "Cookie's" to run on your computer. To enable cookies, consult your browser's help documentation.

2.
Q: My Best Times have been reset
A: The "Minesweeper cookie" has been deleted from your machine. Ensure cookies are enabled (see Question 1.) and your Best Times will be logged again. To avoid your Best Times being reset, do not delete your Minesweeper Cookie.
